Sessions

My work is currently free of charge as I continue to learn and develop myself as a photographer. You will recieve a digital gallery of edited images 2-4 weeks after the session that you can download and share or print as you like!

Engagement

1-hour session
A great way to celebrate your engagement is with a couple's shoot! With a variety of posed and candid photos, I'll walk you through every step with fun and natural prompts to make sure you and your partner feel comfortable in front of the camera. Whether you're looking for a great announcement photo or just looking to cherish this time in your relationship, we'll capture your authentic selves and embrace your love. Open and welcoming to all relationships.

Graduation

1-hour session
Are you graduating high school, college or university this year? Commemorate all your hard work with a graduation photoshoot. Complete with cap and gown, (or use your school's specific regalia!) we'll tour some of your favourite spots on campus. This photoshoot is meant to occur before your graduation date so you recieve your final gallery before the big day. Looking to get photos from the day of your ceremony? Contact me and we can make it happen!

Maternity & Newborn

1-hour session
Honest, emotional, and hearfelt. The aim of this session is to create natural and authentic photos to look back on for years to come. Maternity sessions can be solo shoots or include family. Please note that newborn sessions are for Baby and Parent together.

Lifestyle, Family & Pets

30-60 min session
Life happens fast! Family and lifestyle sessions are the perfect way to capture you and your family in whatever way best represents you, focusing on your relationships and the beauty within. Get camera-shy family members in on the action with easy and comfotable posing and prompts, and finally get a family portrait with everyone in it because YOU'RE not taking the photo!

Holiday Minis

15 min session
Whether it's Valentine's Day, Easter, Mother's/Father's Day, Thanksgiving, Winter Holidays, or your own special day, get a fun themed gallery to celebrate! Great for youngsters and pets with short attention spans. Photoshoots must be scheduled at least 2 weeks prior to the holiday, in order to recieve the finished gallery in time.

Branding

30 min session
Professional headshots and personal branding sessions are available on location or in studio based on your professional needs. Whether you're looking for a new LinkedIn profile picture or a gallery of photos to fill your website, we'll work together to visually embody your brand and messaging.

Is your ideal photoshoot not here?

I am open to all kinds of photo opportunities and would love to discuss ideas with you! Fill out the Contact Form with a short description of the session you're looking for and I'll get back to you ASAP.
