Hello!

Thank you so much for visitng my portfolio. As a new photographer in the Ottawa, Ontario area, you'll notice I only have photos from a few different places, but hope to have more soon! I'm looking for all kinds of photoshoot opportunities, from family and pets, to events and milestones, I want to capture it all for you.

All photoshoots are free of charge and I thank you for your interest and trust in me.
